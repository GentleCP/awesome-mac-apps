Step 3. Compare duplicates

Comparing duplicates in the list view

Click the difference icons to compare duplicates by diverging parameters:

Icon What it shows
Number of times you've played each audio/video file.
Date when you last played a duplicate.
Last duplicate modification dates.
Album titles of audio files.
Artist names.
Song ratings of duplicates.
Application versions.
Size of image duplicates.
Extensions of duplicates.
Sizes of duplicates on disk.
Bit rates of duplicates.
Paths or names of duplicates.
Instances that are marked as loved or favorite.

Comparing duplicates in the grid view

Double-click on a thumbnail to open duplicate details preview. Click on the difference icons to highlight the corresponding instances.
